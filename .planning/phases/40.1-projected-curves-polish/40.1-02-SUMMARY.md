# Phase 40.1-02: Chart Visibility Scoping + Coverage Caption Summary

## Accomplishments

- **Chart visibility scoping landed (PRJ-09).** `CollectionCurveChart` now accepts `drillLevel` + `baselineMode` props (default `'root' / 'rolling'`); `projectionScopeAllows` predicate combines them with `visibleBatchKeys.length`. The projection `<Line>` `hide` prop becomes `!isVisible || !projectionScopeAllows` — second gate stacked on the existing legend-visibility gate, no coupling between the two concerns. `ChartPanel` forwards both props on the preset branch; the generic branch (line/scatter/bar) ignores them since generic charts have no projection.
- **Coverage-absent caption landed (PRJ-10).** When `projectionScopeAllows` is true but the focused batch has no `projection` data, a `<p className="text-caption text-muted-foreground">No modeled curve for this batch</p>` appears as the last child of the DataPanel actions slot. Mirrors the KPI baseline-absent recipe at `kpi-summary-cards.tsx:460` exactly. "Focused batch" resolution: `sortedCurves[0]` at batch-level (single curve in), or `visibleBatchKeys[0]` parsed back to its `sortedCurves` index at partner-level narrowed-to-one.
- **localStorage persistence wired into data-display.tsx (PRJ-12 consumer).** Inline `useState<BaselineMode>('rolling')` swapped to `useBaselineMode()` from Plan 01 — `baselineMode` now persists across sessions at key `'gsd:baselineMode'`. The legacy reset-to-rolling `useEffect` (formerly at lines 298-306) is DELETED, with a 5-line comment marking the deliberate removal so a future reader doesn't reintroduce the symptom. Per-card "Switch to rolling avg" recovery action at `kpi-summary-cards.tsx:462-470` covers partial-coverage; `BaselineSelector`'s `modeledAvailable` disabled state covers full-absent. RESEARCH § Pitfall 8 simpler-alternative path taken.
- **BaselineSelector unification, chart half (PRJ-13).** Both `ChartPanel` mount sites in `data-display.tsx` (partner-level around line 1133, batch-level around line 1147) now receive `drillLevel={drillState.level}` + `baselineMode={baselineMode}`. `BaselineSelector` mount UNCHANGED — still partner-level only (Pitfall 7 Option 2 lock: batch-level projection visibility is decoupled from baselineMode at the predicate level). `onSwitchToRolling` callback at line 1121 now persists via the new setter without further changes.
