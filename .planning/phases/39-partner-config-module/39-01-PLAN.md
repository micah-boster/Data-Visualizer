<objective>
Migrate the entire app from PARTNER_NAME as the selection primary key to (PARTNER_NAME, ACCOUNT_TYPE) pair. This is the gate plan for Phase 39: every other plan in this phase (segment config, list extension, chart/KPI split) depends on pair-aware selection state landing first.

Purpose: PCFG-01..04 enforce "apples-and-oranges" as a correctness property at the selection layer — it must be structurally impossible to view Happy Money's 1st-Party and 3rd-Party data blended together. Today the app treats PARTNER_NAME as unique (it isn't — Happy Money and Zable are multi-product); this plan makes the pair unique and propagates that through drill state, sidebar rendering, root table, cross-partner ranking, anomalies, filter popover, saved-view schema, and the AI context builder.

Output: A pair-aware selection substrate + migrated consumers. Sidebar renders pair rows, drill URL carries ?p=&pr=, filter popover loses the partner combobox, legacy saved views load cleanly via additive-optional schema evolution.
</objective>
