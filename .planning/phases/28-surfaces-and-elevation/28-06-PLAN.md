<objective>
Sweep the overlay family — shadcn primitives (Popover, Sheet, Tooltip, Card, Recharts chart tooltip shell) and inline call-site overlays (query-command-dialog, filter-combobox popup, curve-tooltip, trajectory-tooltip, sort-dialog nested cards) — onto the semantic overlay/floating elevation recipes. Drops conflicting raw `shadow-md`/`shadow-lg`/`shadow-2xl`/`ring-1` defaults that compound with or contradict the new elevation layers.

Purpose: DS-15 requires "popovers and dropdowns use surface-overlay with multi-layer shadows." Today's primitives ship with `shadow-md ring-1 ring-foreground/10` (popover), `shadow-lg` + directional borders (sheet), `shadow-2xl ring-1` (query command dialog), and `shadow-md` (filter combobox, curve tooltip, Recharts chart tooltip). The rim highlight layer inside each semantic elevation replaces the ring; the multi-layer compound replaces the single-layer shadow. Research recommends KEEPING the Tooltip's inverted pill (`bg-foreground text-background`) as a distinct hover-hint affordance — not migrated.

Output: Every overlay across the app renders with a consistent 3-layer compound shadow and surface-overlay (or surface-floating for dialogs/sheets). Cross-primitive visual consistency per DS-17.
</objective>
